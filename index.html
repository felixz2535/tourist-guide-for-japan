Seasons

Spring

When it comes to Spring there is no better activities than cherry blossom viewing. People would gather near cherry blossom trees and have a picnic. You get to spent a great time with your friends, family, or co-workers. Since Golden Week starts from late April to early May it might be really expensive traveling around.

Summer

Summer is when many family would go on vacation. Their destination might be beaches, amusement park really anything. Something that happens quite frequently in Japan is festivals. There are oftens stands that sells food or offers game with reward. During these festival you might be able to see fireworks!!

Fall

Fall is a really refreshing season with the autumn leaves turning red and orange, really a beautiful sight. People might rake up the leaves and have some sweet potato. Scenery in that time is really beautiful, so might as well go to the country sides.

Winter

When it comes to winter you might think of christmas. Your first Christmas in Japan might be weird experience, looking around you would see couple hanging out and people buying KFC and strawberry short cake for their Christmas meals. Around the end of the year comes the new year in which Japanese people do celebrate. They often stay up late to count down to the New Year or climb a mountain to see the first sunrise. And on New Year people often eat Osechi Ryori which is kind of like a bento fill with a lot of dishes that each holds a meaning.
