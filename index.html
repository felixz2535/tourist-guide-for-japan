Japan is considered an interesting country with huge range of activity that appeals to everyone. As a tourist there is just too many things not to do. Seasons

Depending on the season you're visiting Japan there could be some exciting things that is limited to that season.

Spring

When it comes to Spring there is no better activities than cherry blossom viewing. People would gather near cherry blossom trees and have a picnic. You get to spent a great time with your friends, family, or co-workers. Since Golden Week starts from late April to early May it might be really expensive traveling around.

Summer

In Summer many family would go on vacation their destination might be beaches, amusement park really anywhere to spent time having fun, what is special about Japan is that they tend to hold festivals all the time. The festival in the summer is really fun with stands selling food and game that you might win a prize. Plus you can even what fireworks.

Fall

Fall is a really refreshing season with the autumn leaves turning red and orange really a beautiful sight. People might rake up the leaves and have some sweet potato.

Winter

Your first Christmas in Japan might be weird, looking around you would see couple hanging out and people buying KFC and strawberry short cake for their Christmas meals
